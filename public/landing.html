What the Toolkit Covers

The Knowledge Architecture Behind Transition Finance

Each module maps a critical dimension of transition finance — the regulatory framework, the risk logic, the product structure — so that your organisation can build on solid foundations.

Credible Transition Plans

What makes a corporate climate transition plan credible under current EU standards. How to distinguish substantive commitments from greenwashing, and what governance structures underpin a defensible plan.

Transition Risk Assessment

How transition risk propagates through portfolios — top-down exposure analysis and bottom-up driver mapping, aligned with ECB and EBA supervisory expectations.

EU Taxonomy Alignment

The structure of the EU Taxonomy explained: Technical Screening Criteria, DNSH requirements, and minimum social safeguards — broken down by economic activity and environmental objective.

Prudential Framework

How transition risk enters the prudential perimeter: CRR/CRD requirements, EBA guidelines on ESG risks in credit processes, and their implications for capital planning and credit models.

Sustainable Finance Products

The product architecture of transition lending: how sustainability-linked loans, green bonds, and transition-labelled instruments are structured, and what sector-specific KPIs make them credible.

Sector Analysis

What decarbonisation looks like in practice: sector-specific pathways for Steel, Cement, Automotive, and Shipping — including Technology Readiness Levels, stranded asset exposure, and transition benchmarks.
